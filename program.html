The Consistency Flow Program

A proven, systematic approach to building habits that last a lifetime.

The Consistency Flow Timeline

Phase 1: Foundation & Discovery

We begin with a deep dive into your current habits, goals, and challenges. Using the principles of Motivational Interviewing, we'll uncover your intrinsic motivators and set a clear, personalized roadmap.

Phase 2: System Building

This phase is all about implementation. We'll build simple, actionable systems that remove friction and make consistency feel effortless. You'll learn to automate decisions and create triggers for your desired behaviors.

Phase 3: Integration & Mastery

As your new habits solidify, we focus on integrating them into your life for the long term. This phase is about celebrating progress, adapting systems as needed, and building the confidence to maintain your flow indefinitely.
